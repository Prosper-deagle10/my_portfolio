MY START-UP STORY

WHY I CHOOSE DATA ANALYSIS AS A CAREER PATH

WHY DATA ANALYSIS?

Growing up as a tech enthusiast and one with a keen interest in the field of computer programming, venturing into Data Analysis is a dream come through. As an undergraduate, I had passion in solving statistical problems using data. In today's data-driven world, I wanted a career that would allow me to make informed decisions, drive business growth, and tell stories with numbers. Data Analysis stood out as the perfect fit for me.

My Breakthrough!

As we stepped into 2023, I made a bold decision to pivot my career into the exciting world of tech, specifically data analysis. With a thirst for knowledge and a passion for problem-solving, I dove head-first into this new chapter. This journey has not only enhanced my technical abilities but also opened doors to new opportunities and perspectives. I'm eager to continue learning and growing in this field, driving insights and innovation wherever I go.

My Acquired Skills!

Through dedication and perseverance, I've acquired skills in: Data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI. Programming languages like Python and R. Statistical analysis and machine learning techniques. Manipulating databases with SQL and so many others.

Remote Projects!

As a data analyst, I've had the opportunity to work on various remote projects with Dover Insurance. Despite the physical distance, we've achieved remarkable success through collaboration and innovative solutions.

My Certifications!

I'm proud to showcase my Certifications in data analysis from reputable institutions: Simplilearn, Smartech Hub, Uptraxtech, Alex the analyst.
